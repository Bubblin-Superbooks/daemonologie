For where God beginnes iustlie to strike by his lawfull Lieutennentes, it is not in the Deuilles power to defraude or bereaue him of the office, or effect of his powerfull and reuenging Scepter.

Phi. But will neuer their master come to visite them, fra they be once apprehended and put in firmance?

Epi.
